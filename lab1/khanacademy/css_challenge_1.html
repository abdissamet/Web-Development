Сheetah

The cheetah is the fastest land animal in the world. It can run
at speeds of up to 100–120 km/h in short bursts. Cheetahs live mainly in Africa and in a
small part of Iran. They have a slim body, long legs, and black spots that help them blend
into the environment. Cheetahs hunt during the day and mainly eat small to medium-sized animals
such as gazelles.
